What is Financial Warfare?

Financial Warfare is the state of financial conflict that every person carries through their life. Not a crisis, exactly. More like a permanent condition. There are moments when your finances feel solid, and moments when an unexpected medical bill, a job loss, or a pattern of overspending can undo years of progress in a single season.

Financial Warfare does not announce itself. It strikes without warning. And the only way to survive it, let alone win it, is to fight with a Warrior's strategy.

What is Invest Like a Warrior?

Founded at a tabletop, Invest Like a Warrior is a financial coaching and education platform built to help you uncover your hidden Warrior potential.

The platform is built around six unique Financial Warrior classes. Each class carries its own identity, its own Power Object, and its own set of financial principles and goals to live by. Together they form a complete system for understanding how you think about money, risk, and wealth building.

Leveling up your finances is no different than running a campaign. ILAW takes the best of Dungeons and Dragons, Warhammer, and tabletop strategy, and combines it with asset classes, financial planning, and real coaching. The result is the most complete nerd-based financial education resource ever built.

Your financial life is a campaign. It is time to fight like a Warrior.

Meet Your Coach

Jamie Hoffman

Jamie Hoffman is the founder and Chief Financial Warlord of Invest Like a Warrior.

Jamie grew up in the Midwest, America's tabletop gaming heartland. In college, he hit rock bottom financially. He was chasing too many goals and drowning in conflicting advice: Dave Ramsey said debt was dumb, Grant Cardone said debt was stronger than cash, Tai Lopez said it was all about scaling a brand. The noise was overwhelming.

One night, out of desperation, Jamie grabbed a stack of financial education books and his Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook and started writing. What emerged was an outline that became the Financial Warrior System.

From there, Jamie sat down with financial professionals across the spectrum: Series 65/66 advisors, CFPs, life insurance agents, business leaders, crypto enthusiasts, and day traders, all of whom helped shape the system from a player character sheet into a fully fledged financial education and coaching platform built to stand the test of time and truly change people's financial lives.

ILAW is a financial education platform. Jamie Hoffman is not a certified financial advisor. Nothing on this site constitutes personalized financial advice.
