About

Jamie Hoffman

Jamie Hoffman didn't set out to build a financial education platform. He set out to survive. Facing eviction in his junior year of college, working 30 hours a week at near-minimum wage, and caring for his mother as her health declined, he found himself buried under contradictory advice and overwhelming noise.

So he did what any self-respecting nerd would do. He grabbed his Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, sat it next to a stack of personal finance books, and started writing. What emerged was a framework that mapped every warrior class to a different financial personality — and made the game finally feel winnable.

Jamie spent 15 years in digital marketing and has worked deep in the AI space. He is based in the Midwest — the quiet heartland of the tabletop gaming world — and believes that in an age of artificial everything, there is something irreplaceable about real warriors gathering to build real wealth together.

ILAW is a financial education platform. Jamie Hoffman is not a certified financial advisor. Nothing on this site constitutes personalized financial advice.
